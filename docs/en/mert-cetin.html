About

Mert Çetin (XertXetin) is a Turkish technology expert and entrepreneur known as the founder of Me Force, which provides innovative solutions in artificial intelligence, big data, IoT, and cloud computing. Introduced to computers and technology at a young age, Mert quickly rose to leadership positions in his career, which he began as a software engineer. As CEO of Me Force, he has led projects like the social media platform Salyap and the IFOX Web3 browser. Additionally, he has developed infrastructures such as CetinJS, CetinDB, and CetinServer, bringing unique technological solutions to his projects.

Mert Çetin (XertXetin) has won numerous awards and certificates for his contributions to the tech industry. Beyond his technology career, he is also involved in AI-assisted music production, with his songs available on platforms like Spotify and Apple Music. Mentoring young entrepreneurs, Mert shares his expertise in technology and innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of creativity.

Who is Mert Çetin? (b. July 21, 1997, Istanbul), a Turkish technology expert, entrepreneur, and the founder of the Me Force brand. He graduated with distinction from Avcılar Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School. In 2012, he launched the Me Force initiative, and by 2014, he decided to fully dedicate himself to his business, choosing not to pursue further university education.

Career

Mert Çetin grew up in an environment where his family was deeply involved with technology and computers. This exposure allowed him to become familiar with computers and technology from a young age, developing his interest and skills even by playing computer games at 4-5 years old.

He worked in fields such as IT, computer sales, repair, and network services. During this time, he gained experience by working on small-scale projects like games and simple programs before engaging directly in software development.

Starting his career as a software engineer, Mert quickly advanced to leadership roles in various projects. In 2014, he founded Me Force, taking significant steps toward developing innovative and groundbreaking technology solutions. Mert Çetin specializes in areas such as artificial intelligence, big data, cloud computing, and IoT and is proficient in numerous programming languages, including Node.js, PHP, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, Python, C, and Assembly.

Mert Çetin has managed this career transformation by staying in sync with the rapid changes in the tech world. As the leader of Me Force, he continues to spearhead projects that push the boundaries of technology, focusing on providing innovative tech solutions and shaping the technologies of the future.

Unknown Projects

One of the less visible but highly important aspects of Mert Çetin's career is his work with his own developed infrastructures. These infrastructures consist of three main systems: CetinJS, CetinDB, and CetinServer. They are unique infrastructure systems designed by Mert Çetin himself.

CetinJS is a JavaScript (vanilla js) based infrastructure used primarily for developing mobile and web applications. CetinDB, on the other hand, serves as a database management system (designed to process large and fast data sets) and is used for data storage and access. Finally, CetinServer provides fast and secure management of web servers and APIs.

These infrastructures play a critical role in bringing Mert Çetin's projects to life. Thanks to the infrastructures he developed, he can manage and develop his projects more efficiently and reliably. Furthermore, these infrastructures are entirely developed by Mert Çetin and are kept closed-source, only being used in his own projects.

His decision to develop these infrastructures highlights his dedication to technology and his innovative approach. These infrastructures are key elements that make his projects unique and provide a competitive advantage.

CetinJS

High FPS Fast Virtual Scroll

CetinJS is a JavaScript-based framework that, although similar to frameworks like React, Ionic, and others, has a key difference: CetinJS is developed entirely using vanilla JavaScript (pure JS). This framework does not contain any unnecessary commands and is equipped only with efficient commands needed for optimal performance. Every new command added is rigorously tested and approved before being included in the framework. This ensures that performance and reliability are maximized. CetinJS enables users to develop faster and more efficient applications.

CetinDB

High-Speed 10000K Data Insertion

CetinDB is a database management system designed for storing large and fast data sets. It has a structure similar to LevelDB and MongoDB but is optimized for performance and efficiency. CetinDB offers high-speed data access and low latency. While working with large data sets, it maintains the consistency and accessibility of the data. This system plays a significant role, especially in large-scale applications and projects that require high data traffic.

CetinServer

Fast Data Loading

CetinServer is a scalable server system built on Node.js. This system hosts components like Socket.io, efficiently managing real-time communication and data transfer. CetinServer offers high-performance and reliable server infrastructure. Additionally, with its scalable architecture, it can be easily expanded to meet increasing user and data demands. This server system plays a critical role in Mert Çetin's projects, ensuring that various applications run smoothly and reliably.
